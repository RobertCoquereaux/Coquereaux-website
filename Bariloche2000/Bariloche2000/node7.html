Housing

  • The Centro Atómico Bariloche contains a number of rooms that will be free at that time (because of summer hollidays in the austral hemisphere). These rooms, as a rule, are units that contain two beds and one bathroom and they turn out to be rather cheap: a contribution of $4 a day will be asked (by the CAB) for each room. However, it is to be expected that there will not be enough of them for everybody. This meeting being a school and, in particular a CIMPA school --see this link-- it is clear that priority will be given to students (doctoral or post doctoral students), and more particularly to those students coming from latino-american countries. For those room assignments, priority will also be given to those who are willing to share their room with another participant.

  • Participants who will not be housed within the CAB, either because they could not be accepted (for lack of space) or for other reasons, will be housed within a hotel situated at a walking distance from the CAB (15 minutes), along the lake. Cost will be around $25 for a single room (breakfast included). For a double, it would be...the double. There are rooms with three or four beds (for those coming with their family). Of course the prices will be discussed again and made more precise when the time of the school comes near. Reservations should be made enough time in advance (three months) and some percentage of the payment will be required, ahead of time, by the hotel (this can be made with virtually any kind of credit card).

  • There are many other possibilities of housing in this area, from small bungalows --or tents-- within the woods to four stars hotels within the village, and with prices ranging from almost nothing to $150. Renting a bungalow (about $80 a day) may also be a convenient solution for a family. As a rule, organizers will help with priority those participants who accept to be housed within the CAB or within the previously mentioned hotel. For housing questions, please contact us by email: b2000@cabtep2.cnea.gov.ar.

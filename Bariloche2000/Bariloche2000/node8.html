Food

  • Breakfast can be taken at the CAB cafeteria for those people housed at the CAB. Others may take it at their hotel.
  • Lunch will be served everyday at the CAB cafeteria (modic price, around $4).
  • Dinner is not served at the CAB. There are several possibilities for eating in one of the restaurants located in the area (15 minutes walk). Prices are around $10. Of course there are many other possibilities -for instance sandwiches, etc.- in the village itself, 10 kilometers away. Several regular buses (lines 10, 20, 21) run until late at night and, if needed, they are taxis or ``remis'' (private taxis) at any time.

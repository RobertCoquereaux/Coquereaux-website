Schedule

  • We shall have three lectures of two hours everyday, four hours in the morning and two in the late afternoon.
  • It is too early to know the precise schedule of lectures. However the timing will be more or less the following: lectures will start every day at 8:30 am and stop at 1 pm, with a 30 minutes break. Most of the afternoon will be free but there will be a last lecture from 6:30 to 8:30 pm. Note: it is anyway almost impossible to get a dinner before 9 pm in the area.
  • As a rule, each lecturer will deliver courses of six hours splitted in three lectures of two hours. Usually the three talks of every lecturer will be given during three consecutive (working) days.
  • Since we have nine lecturers, this makes 54 hours and therefore 9 days of lectures, 6 hours a day. For this reason, there will be lectures every day during the first week (Monday 10 to Friday 15) but we shall start only on Tuesday the second week (Tuesday 19 to Friday 22). This extended weekend of three days should allow the interested foreign visitors to participate in one of the many possible excursions that are possible within this part of Patagonia (many such excursions require usually two full days).

School versus conference

This is a school, not a conference. The two weeks of the school will be mostly taken by the lectures. Of course, there will be some time, ``after hours'', or during the otherwise ``free'' time, to organize informal talks, and some participants should be able to speak about their own work. However, these activities will be usually organized by the interested participants themselves, and in any case, at a later stage.

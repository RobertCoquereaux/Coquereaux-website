Financial support and candidatures

  • All candidatures should be sent to CIMPA (see the address here). Please, print and fill in the application form, and send it together with the documents there requested.

  • Independently of the housing facilities offered by the Centro Atómico Bariloche, there will be available some --limited-- financial support, including support for travel expenses, coming from different sources, and handled by the directors of the school. Potential participants should first look for financial help within their own home institutions. If this fails they should ask for advise from one of the members of the organizing committee. If everything else fails they can get in touch directly with one of the directors of the school.

  • Candidates should specify in their application letter the kind of financial support that they could get apart from the potential one provided by the school. Although things can certainly be modified at a later stage, potential participants should clearly state their preferences about lodging (CAB shared room, hotel...). Those who think that they will come with a spouse (or family) are also asked to say so. Organizers need indeed to know some time in advance the number of rooms that they will have to reserve in an hotel in the neighbourhood.
  • The lecture hall offered by the CAB is quite big... so size is not an issue. However, the number of participants will be limited by the housing facilities and by the wish of the organizers who want to keep the size of the school reasonable.
  • The deadline for applications is October 29, 1999. Participants should certainly not wait that long before sending their application. It is indeed to be expected that most available fellowships will be attributed by then. Of course there are exceptions: fellowships offered by some institutions may not be made official before the end of 1999. This should not prevent the interested people to send their application to CIMPA while explicitly mentionning that they expect to receive some kind of financial support at a later stage.
  • Participants from some countries will need some kind of ``official'' letter of invitation in order to be able to ask for a financial support from their own home institutions. Such letters can directly be obtained from the directors of the school.

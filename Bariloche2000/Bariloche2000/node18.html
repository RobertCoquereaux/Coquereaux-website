CIMPA (International Centre for Pure and Applied Mathematics)

CIMPA is a non-profit international organization established in Nice (France). Its aim is to promote international cooperation to the benefit of developing countries, with respect to higher education and research in Mathematics and related subjects like Theoretical Physics or Computer Science.

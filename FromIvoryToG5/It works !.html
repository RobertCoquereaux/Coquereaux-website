Once the connection is established, you can launch as many activities as you want on the G5 side.
The connection (display) is fast : it takes actually less time to update or scroll a X11 window on the remote display (on the G5) than to do it on the main Ivory console attached to the FX
Of course it is also nicer  : Ivory panes look much better on an Apple Cinema HD display than on an twenty years old 15” monitor. Actually I can now even launch activities like Concordia that could not be launched on small displays (my main Ivory console is unfortunately very small)
If you want your FX to relax after this feast,  you may shut down everything, but before that you need to exit X11 from any listener (at the main console, or from the secondary listener) by typing :
Halt X Screen
